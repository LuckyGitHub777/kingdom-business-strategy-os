# Kingdom Business Strategy OS

**A practical operating system for founders, owners, executives, and steward-leaders building real businesses with discipline, service, trust, and long-term value.**

This repository converts business-simulation lessons and real-world operating principles into reusable doctrine, checklists, templates, examples, review loops, and an operating cadence.

**Kingdom building** here means service, stewardship, trust, excellence, and responsible increase. The material is open to anyone who wants to build useful, ethical, disciplined, and durable organizations.

Maintained as a public personal GitHub project. This repository is focused on practical doctrine for business strategy, decision quality, operating cadence, evidence-based forecasting, public-safe frontier readiness, and repeatable learning loops.

> A strong business is not built by luck, noise, or one clever idea. It is built by serving a specific customer, creating real value, protecting cash, preserving margin, forecasting from evidence, improving operations, earning trust, and learning every cycle.

## Operating cadence

This OS becomes operational when used on a rhythm:

| Frequency | Tool | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Weekly | [After-Action Review](templates/06_after_action_review_template.md) | Convert results into system updates. |
| Weekly or monthly | [Forecast Assumptions Register](templates/03_forecast_assumptions_register_template.md) | Check demand, cash, timing, and downside risk. |
| Monthly | [Operational Excellence Scorecard](templates/05_operational_excellence_scorecard_template.md) | Reduce waste and improve delivery. |
| Monthly or quarterly | [Business Model Review](templates/01_business_model_review_template.md) | Validate customer, value, cost, margin, and scale conditions. |
| Before major decisions | [Decision Quality Checklist](templates/04_decision_quality_checklist_template.md) | Prevent hope-based spending, hiring, expansion, or product moves. |
| Before building or selling | [Customer Segment Brief](templates/02_customer_segment_brief_template.md) | Match the offer to a specific buyer. |

For the full sequence, see the [Operating Cadence](docs/05-operating-cadence.md) and [Canonical Taxonomy](docs/06-canonical-taxonomy.md).

## Core doctrine

The OS uses ten connected doctrines:

1. **Service creates purpose**: Business begins with useful value.
2. **Stewardship protects increase**: Resources must be managed with discipline.
3. **Cash is oxygen**: Cash timing matters before pressure becomes crisis.
4. **Margin reveals truth**: Revenue creates motion, but margin reveals health.
5. **Timing converts capability into value**: A strong offer can fail if it arrives late.
6. **Forecasts must follow evidence**: Hope is not a forecast.
7. **Inventory is trapped capital**: Unused capacity quietly drains strength.
8. **Operational excellence compounds**: Better systems reduce waste over time.
9. **Trust is strategic capital**: Trust lowers friction and supports scale.
10. **Learning loops create mastery**: Every result contains information.

Read the full doctrine in [Business Strategy for Kingdom Building](docs/01-business-strategy-for-kingdom-building.md).
