# Quickstart: First 15 Minutes

This is the fastest way to use the Kingdom Business Strategy OS.

## Goal

Get from reading to action in one short operating loop.

## Step 1: Pick one offer or project

Choose one business idea, offer, product, service, campaign, or decision. Do not review the whole company at once.

Write one sentence:

> We are reviewing: ____________________________

## Step 2: Fill three fields

Open the [Business Model Review Template](templates/01_business_model_review_template.md) and answer only:

- Customer
- Problem
- Evidence of demand

Use the [Example Business Model Review](examples/01_example_business_model_review.md) as a model.

## Step 3: Run the first evidence check

Ask:

1. Who exactly values this?
2. What proof shows they need it?
3. What would make us wrong?
4. How does this support service, stewardship, and long-term trust?

If the answers are weak, use the [Customer Segment Brief Template](templates/02_customer_segment_brief_template.md) next.

## Step 4: Create one next action

Write one action with an owner and date:

| Action | Owner | Due date | Evidence expected |
|---|---|---|---|
| | | | |

## Step 5: Close the loop

After the action, complete the [After-Action Review Template](templates/06_after_action_review_template.md). The OS works only when results become learning and learning becomes system updates.

## Path after the first 15 minutes

| Situation | Use this |
|---|---|
| We need to understand the whole offer. | [Business Model Review Template](templates/01_business_model_review_template.md) |
| We do not know the customer clearly. | [Customer Segment Brief Template](templates/02_customer_segment_brief_template.md) |
| We are about to produce, hire, spend, or expand. | [Forecast Assumptions Register Template](templates/03_forecast_assumptions_register_template.md) |
| We are making a major decision. | [Decision Quality Checklist Template](templates/04_decision_quality_checklist_template.md) |
| Delivery feels slow, expensive, or messy. | [Operational Excellence Scorecard Template](templates/05_operational_excellence_scorecard_template.md) |
| Something happened and we need to learn. | [After-Action Review Template](templates/06_after_action_review_template.md) |
