## Public-use boundary

This document is educational and strategic-planning material. It is not legal, cybersecurity, procurement, accounting, tax, investment, or compliance advice. Regulated or high-risk implementation should be reviewed with qualified professionals.

## Core positioning

> We help organizations adopt frontier technology without losing trust, security, evidence, or control.

The opportunity is not hype. The opportunity is trust infrastructure.

## Solution 1: AI Automation Readiness and Implementation

For organizations that know AI matters but do not know where to start.

Potential deliverables may include:

- Workflow audit
- Automation opportunity map
- Risk and control review
- AI tool selection guide
- SOP automation plan
- Agent governance checklist
- Pilot automation build
- Executive dashboard

## Solution 2: Cyber-AI Resilience Operating Model

For organizations adopting AI that need security, governance, and mission continuity.

Potential deliverables may include:

- AI risk register
- Sensitive-data exposure review
- AI usage policy
- AI agent access-control model
- Incident-response playbook
- Vendor questionnaire
- Executive cyber-AI board report
- Audit evidence package

## Solution 3: Quantum-Safe Readiness Index

For organizations preparing for post-quantum cryptography, long-lived data risk, and infrastructure migration.

Potential deliverables may include:

- Cryptographic asset inventory
- Long-lived data exposure map
- Vendor quantum-risk questionnaire
- PQC migration roadmap
- Executive readiness score
- Phased implementation plan
- Policy and compliance brief

## Solution 4: Frontier Technology Bottleneck and Investment Map

For investors, founders, labs, agencies, and strategic partners working across quantum, AI, materials, space, cyber, sensing, and critical supply chains.

Potential deliverables may include:

- Technology lifecycle map
- Bottleneck analysis
- Materials and supply-chain risk review
- Builder maturity assessment
- Validation and testbed requirements
- Partner ecosystem map
- Investment-priority memo

## Solution 5: Evidence Governance System

For institutions dealing with high-uncertainty public-interest evidence, advanced sensing data, aerospace events, contested claims, scientific uncertainty, or public trust risk.

Potential deliverables may include:

- Evidence classification model
- Source reliability scoring
- Sensor/data chain-of-custody template
- Claim confidence matrix
- Public communication guidance
- Scientific review workflow
- Misinformation/disinformation risk review
- Archive and research protocol
