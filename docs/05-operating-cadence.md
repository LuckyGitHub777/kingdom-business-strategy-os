## Core cadence

| Frequency | Template | Output | Feeds |
|---|---|---|---|
| Weekly | [After-Action Review](../templates/06_after_action_review_template.md) | Lessons and system updates | Issue forms, SOPs, forecasts |
| Weekly or monthly | [Forecast Assumptions Register](../templates/03_forecast_assumptions_register_template.md) | Base/downside/upside cases | Cash, hiring, production, spending decisions |
| Monthly | [Operational Excellence Scorecard](../templates/05_operational_excellence_scorecard_template.md) | Top waste or risk reduction action | Process improvements |
| Monthly or quarterly | [Business Model Review](../templates/01_business_model_review_template.md) | Offer health and scale conditions | Strategy review |
| Before major decisions | [Decision Quality Checklist](../templates/04_decision_quality_checklist_template.md) | Go/revise/pause/kill decision | After-action review |
| Before building or selling | [Customer Segment Brief](../templates/02_customer_segment_brief_template.md) | Segment-specific buyer clarity | Offer, message, pricing |

| Trigger | Use this first | Then use |
|---|---|---|

| Unclear customer | [Customer Segment Brief](../templates/02_customer_segment_brief_template.md) | [Business Model Review](../templates/01_business_model_review_template.md) |
| Hiring, spending, production, or expansion | [Forecast Assumptions Register](../templates/03_forecast_assumptions_register_template.md) | [Decision Quality Checklist](../templates/04_decision_quality_checklist_template.md) |

## Monthly operating review

Agenda:

1. Review last month’s [After-Action Reviews](../templates/06_after_action_review_template.md).
2. Update one [Forecast Assumptions Register](../templates/03_forecast_assumptions_register_template.md).
3. Score operations with the [Operational Excellence Scorecard](../templates/05_operational_excellence_scorecard_template.md).
4. Pick one system improvement.
5. Assign owner, due date, and review date.

## Quarterly strategy review

Agenda:

1. Re-run the [Business Model Review](../templates/01_business_model_review_template.md).
2. Re-run the [Customer Segment Brief](../templates/02_customer_segment_brief_template.md) for the top customer segment.
3. Review cash, margin, delivery, trust, and risk assumptions.
4. Decide what to continue, revise, pause, or kill.
5. Update the roadmap, templates, or operating cadence if reality has changed.

## Frontier technology cadence

Use this lightweight cadence when applying the OS to AI automation, cyber-AI resilience, quantum-safe readiness, supply-chain stewardship, or high-uncertainty evidence governance.

| Rhythm | Review | Tool |
|---|---|---|
| Before starting | Define the use case, buyer, and risk boundary. | [Business Model Review](../templates/01_business_model_review_template.md), [Decision Quality Checklist](../templates/04_decision_quality_checklist_template.md) |
| Weekly during pilot | Capture what changed, what broke, and what needs a control. | [After-Action Review](../templates/06_after_action_review_template.md) |
| Monthly | Re-check assumptions, evidence, cost, risk, and delivery readiness. | [Forecast Assumptions Register](../templates/03_forecast_assumptions_register_template.md), [Operational Excellence Scorecard](../templates/05_operational_excellence_scorecard_template.md) |
| Quarterly | Decide whether to continue, revise, pause, or expand. | [Business Model Review](../templates/01_business_model_review_template.md) |
| Before public claims | Check evidence, confidence, public-use boundary, and governance risk. | [Frontier Readiness Starter Checklists](08-frontier-readiness-starter-checklists.md) |
