# Business Simulation to Real Business Lessons

Business simulations compress real-world consequences into a small number of rounds. The lesson is not just how to win a simulation. The lesson is how businesses fail, recover, learn, and become disciplined.

## Core lesson

A business simulation is not only a product game. It is a business-system game.

A team can have a better product and still lose if it misreads demand, launches improvements too late, overproduces, takes bad debt, ignores cost structure, or fails to learn from reports.

## Master loop

```text
Read → Decide → Forecast → Execute → Compare → Diagnose → Document → Improve
```

The organization that learns faster gains advantage.

1. Complete the [After-Action Review Template](../templates/06_after_action_review_template.md).
