# Frontier Readiness Starter Checklists

This document gives a public-safe starter layer for teams applying the Kingdom Business Strategy OS to AI automation, cyber-AI resilience, quantum-safe readiness, supply-chain stewardship, and high-uncertainty evidence governance.

It is intentionally lightweight. It helps teams ask better first questions before creating private implementation plans, regulated assurance work, or client-specific deliverables.

## Public-use boundary

These checklists are educational and planning-oriented. They are not legal, cybersecurity, procurement, accounting, tax, investment, compliance, or engineering advice. Regulated or high-risk work should be reviewed with qualified professionals.

Do not put sensitive system details, credentials, protected personal information, client data, confidential architecture, or proprietary operational details into public issues or pull requests.

## How to use these checklists

For any frontier initiative:

1. Pick one checklist.
2. Answer only what is knowable now.
3. Mark unknowns as assumptions.
4. Move the assumptions into the [Forecast Assumptions Register Template](../templates/03_forecast_assumptions_register_template.md).
5. Use the [Decision Quality Checklist Template](../templates/04_decision_quality_checklist_template.md) before spending, hiring, building, publishing, or integrating.
6. Close the loop with the [After-Action Review Template](../templates/06_after_action_review_template.md).
