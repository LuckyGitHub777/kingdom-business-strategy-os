# Example Forecast Assumptions Register

## Forecast name

AI automation audit pilot demand forecast.

## Time horizon

30 days.

## Base case

Three warm outreach conversations produce one paid pilot.

## Downside case

Three conversations produce interest but no payment because the offer is unclear or the buyer does not feel urgency.

## Upside case

Three conversations produce two paid pilots or one pilot plus a referral.

## Key assumptions

| Assumption | Evidence | Confidence | What breaks if wrong? | Owner |
|---|---|---:|---|---|
| Warm contacts have current AI confusion. | Prior conversations mention AI interest. | 3 | Offer timing may be weak. | Founder |
| A fixed-fee audit is easier to buy than open-ended consulting. | Buyer wants clarity and cost control. | 4 | Pricing may need revision. | Founder |
| Delivery can be completed in 12 to 25 hours. | Template-driven scope. | 3 | Margin may weaken. | Delivery lead |
| Security concerns are a buying driver. | Buyers worry about data handling. | 4 | Messaging must shift to productivity. | Founder |

## Demand evidence

Warm network conversations, common AI adoption confusion, and internal process pain.

## Cost evidence

Estimated delivery hours based on workflow audit, interview, and report template.

## Cash timing evidence

Require payment before final report delivery.

## Competitor / market evidence

Generic AI consulting is available, but many offers are broad and hype-driven.

## Decision trigger

Whether to create the one-page offer sheet and start outreach.

## Review cadence

Review after three outreach conversations or 14 days, whichever comes first.
