# Example Operational Excellence Scorecard

## Scorecard

| Area | Score | Evidence | Improvement action | Owner |
|---|---:|---|---|---|
| Training | 2 | Delivery knowledge lives in founder's head. | Create delivery checklist. | Founder |
| Documentation | 3 | Templates exist. | Add sample final report. | Founder |
| Quality control | 2 | No review checklist yet. | Add pre-delivery QA checklist. | Delivery lead |
| Automation | 2 | Manual document assembly. | Create reusable report outline. | Founder |
| Cross-functional coordination | 3 | Small team, informal coordination. | Add owner and due date fields. | Ops lead |
| Tooling | 3 | Standard docs and templates usable. | Standardize folder naming. | Ops lead |
| Data quality | 2 | Intake answers may vary. | Add intake form with required fields. | Founder |
| Speed | 3 | Scope is focused. | Timebox discovery. | Delivery lead |
| Rework reduction | 2 | No lessons captured yet. | Run AAR after pilot. | Founder |
| Customer handoff | 2 | Final handoff not standardized. | Create handoff email and summary. | Ops lead |
| Risk controls | 3 | Public-use boundary exists. | Add data-handling note. | Founder |
| Learning loop | 3 | AAR template exists. | Schedule review before kickoff. | Founder |

## Highest leverage improvement

Create a standard final report outline and QA checklist before the first paid pilot.

## Owner and due date

- Owner: Founder
- Due date: Next Friday
- Next review date: Two weeks after first pilot kickoff
