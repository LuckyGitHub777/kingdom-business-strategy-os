# Business Model Review Template

Use this to diagnose whether an offer can create value, serve a specific customer, return more value than it costs to deliver, and scale without chaos.

## How to fill this in

Use plain evidence. Short answers are better than vague essays. Unknown is an acceptable answer if it triggers a next action.

## Offer

What is being sold?

## Customer

Who values it most?

## Problem

What problem, need, duty, risk, or goal does it address?

## Value

Why does the customer care?

## Evidence of demand

What proves that customers want or need this?

## Price

What will customers pay?

## Full cost to deliver

Include labor, software, materials, support, management, compliance, sales, onboarding, and overhead.

## Margin

What gross, contribution, and net margin may remain?

## Delivery model

How is the promise fulfilled?

## Risk

What can break the model?

## Repeatability

Can the business deliver this repeatedly without heroic effort?

## Scale conditions

What must be true before expansion?

## Mission and stewardship alignment

How does this offer support useful service, responsible stewardship, ethical increase, and long-term trust? What boundary keeps it from becoming extractive, confusing, or wasteful?

## Decision

- Continue
- Revise
- Pause
- Kill

## Next action

What happens next, who owns it, and by when?
