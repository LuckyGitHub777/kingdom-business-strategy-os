# Operational Excellence Scorecard Template

Use this monthly to reduce waste, strengthen delivery, and make quality improvements visible.

## How to fill this in

Score each area from 1 to 5 using the anchors below. Add evidence, not just opinion.

## Scoring anchors

| Score | Anchor |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Ad hoc, undocumented, person-dependent, or frequently broken |
| 2 | Partly defined, inconsistent, and reactive |
| 3 | Documented and usable, but not consistently measured or improved |
| 4 | Measured, owned, and improving with visible controls |
| 5 | Reliable, repeatable, measured, trained, and continuously improved |
