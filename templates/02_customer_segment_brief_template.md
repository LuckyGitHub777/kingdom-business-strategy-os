# Customer Segment Brief Template

Use this before building, selling, pricing, or messaging. A strong segment brief prevents the business from building for a vague average customer.

## How to fill this in

Describe one segment only. If there are multiple segments, create a separate brief for each.

## Segment name

## Buyer description

Who are they?

## Core problem

What pain, duty, goal, risk, or desire do they have?

## Buying criteria

What do they value most?

- Price
- Speed
- Reliability
- Compliance
- Security
- Novelty
- Ease
- Status
- Support
- Evidence

## Budget and willingness to pay

What can they realistically pay?

## Proof required

What evidence do they need before buying?

## Decision process

Who decides, who influences, and who blocks?

## Timing window

When do they buy or act?

## Alternatives

What do they use now?

## Message

What clear promise should be made to this segment?

## Risks

What would make this segment hard to serve?
