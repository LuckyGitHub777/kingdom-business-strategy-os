# Decision Quality Checklist Template

Use before major spending, hiring, product, pricing, capital, partnership, or expansion decisions.

## How to fill this in

Write the decision clearly, score each dimension, and name the evidence. A low score does not automatically block action, but it forces risk awareness.

## Scoring anchors

| Score | Anchor |
|---:|---|
| 1 | Unknown, unsupported, or mostly hope-based |
| 2 | Some evidence, but assumptions are weak or untested |
| 3 | Adequate evidence and known risks, but gaps remain |
| 4 | Strong evidence, clear owner, and realistic mitigation |
| 5 | Proven by repeated evidence, measured, and ready to execute |
