# Code of Conduct

This project is dedicated to disciplined, respectful, and constructive business learning.

Participants are expected to:

- Communicate respectfully.
- Improve the work rather than attack people.
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, or abusive conduct.
- Keep discussions focused on practical value, evidence, and learning.
- Respect confidentiality and public/private boundaries.
- Avoid unsupported claims or guaranteed-outcome language.

For sensitive reports, follow the [Security Policy](SECURITY.md).
